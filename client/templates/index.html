Welcome to the
Design for America
Collaborative

The DFA Collaborative is a network of amazing leaders - DFA students, alumni and like-minded professionals - working to make this world a better place through design and social impact.

We inspire each other to take chances.

We realize growth comes through taking inspiration and going for it. We share stories on how to translate design thinking and social impact to new sectors.

We support each other to find fun, meaningful work.

We support passion projects, making a difference, giving back and expanding into more awesomeness. As an active, collaborative community, we are here to help you in your search for meaningful work by providing resources, networks and mentorship.
